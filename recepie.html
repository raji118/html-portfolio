Raji's Omelette recepie

Ingredients

Item required

  • Eggs 2
  • Onions
  • Carrot

Powders

  • Turmeric powder
  • Chilly powder
    • chillies
    • powder
  • Salt

Procedure

  1. Cut the onions and mix all the required powders in the onions
  2. Beat the eggs into the onions

  4. take the Omelette
